Sustainability in Fashion on a Personal Level

BRIDGET LESLIE

For every piece of clothing I wore I determined where it came from, who made it, what it is made from, why I purchased it, and whether or not it was an ethical purchase. I concluded that since most of the clothing I own is unethically produced, and the vast majority of brands use unethical business practices, the best way to shop sustainably is to limit your personal consumption.
